04 / About

Eyes trained on the horizon.

I'm a landscape and mountain photographer based wherever the next expedition takes me. My work lives at the intersection of endurance and light — the images that only appear when you're willing to carry a 15kg pack to 4,000 metres.

Shooting primarily in the Alps, Dolomites, and Patagonia, I specialize in the moments between — not the summit, but the approach. Not the sunrise, but the blue hour before it.
